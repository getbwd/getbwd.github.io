BWD Services — Genesee County, MI

BWD Services was built from the ground up — literally. We didn't start with a truck or a trailer or a garage full of equipment. We started with a lawn mower crammed in the back seat of a car and nowhere to go but forward.

A lawn mower in the back seat of a car.

How BWD started

We started this business because we wanted to build something real. Something ours. Not a side project. Not something to put on a college application. A real business, built with real work.

We're still in high school. We're still figuring it out. And we're still pulling equipment out of the back seat to get to every job on time. That part hasn't changed — and we don't want it to.

Every lawn we cut, every driveway we clear — it's not just a service call. It's proof that you don't need perfect circumstances to do good work. You just need to show up.

That's what BWD is. And that's what it'll keep being.
