Our Story

How Bros Who Do became a year-round property service company.

BWD, short for Bros Who Do, was founded on one simple idea: show up, work hard, and do the job right.


What started as local lawn care quickly evolved into a full year-round property service company. We saw a problem in Michigan — homeowners constantly switching between different companies for mowing, fall cleanups, and winter snow removal.


So we built one trusted team to handle it all.

What We Do

Complete seasonal services under one reliable team.

Spring & Summer

Lawn mowing, trimming, edging, landscaping upgrades, and full property cleanups to keep your exterior sharp.

Fall Services

Leaf removal, seasonal clearing, gutter cleanouts, and winter preparation to protect your property.

Winter Services

Snow removal, salting, ice management, and emergency plowing during heavy Michigan storms.
