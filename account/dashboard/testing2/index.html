BWD — Jobs Firestore Seeder

Dev tool · Seeds sample appointment documents into the jobs collection.

Document Schema — jobs/{jobId}
jobIdstring — document ID, e.g. "JOB-0042" uidstring — Firebase Auth UID of the customer statusstring — "Scheduled" | "Completed" | "Cancelled" | "Rescheduled" dateTimestamp — service date timestring — e.g. "9:00 AM – 11:00 AM" addressstring — service address lineItemsarray of { name, qty, unitPrice, lineTotal } totalnumber — sum of all lineItem lineTotals invoiceIdstring — linked invoice ID, e.g. "INV-0091" assignedCrewstring — crew member name(s) customerNotesstring — notes left by the customer crewNotesstring — notes left by the crew after the visit ratingnumber — 1–5 star rating (0 = not yet rated) feedbackstring — written feedback from customer createdAtTimestamp — when the job record was created
